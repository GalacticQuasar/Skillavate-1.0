Skillavate Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 9/30/2023

Welcome to Skillavate! This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our website. Please read this Policy carefully to understand how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

a. User-Provided Information: We gather contact information and other details provided by Tutors, including names, email addresses, phone numbers, location information, and lesson descriptions. We collect this information with the explicit consent of Tutors.

b. Automatically Collected Information: When you access Skillavate, we may automatically collect certain information, including your IP address, device type, browser type, and browsing behavior. We use cookies and similar technologies for this purpose. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

To provide and improve our services.
To facilitate contact between Tutors and Students.
To personalize your experience and deliver relevant content.
To analyze website usage and trends.
To communicate with you regarding your use of Skillavate.
To protect the security and integrity of our platform.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information with the following parties:

Tutors: We display your contact information to Students interested in contacting you for lessons.
Legal and Safety: We may disclose your information when required by law or to protect the safety, rights, or property of Skillavate, its users, or others.
Service Providers: We may share your information with service providers who help us operate the website.
Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of Skillavate, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no data transmission over the internet or stored on a server can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

5. Your Choices

You can control certain data by updating your profile or contacting us directly. You can also opt-out of certain communications. However, please note that you cannot opt-out of essential service-related communications.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties, so we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy on our website with the last updated date. Your continued use of Skillavate after any changes to this Policy will constitute your acceptance of those changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at skillavate@gmail.com  

By using Skillavate, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy.
